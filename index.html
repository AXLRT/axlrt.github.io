About

AXLRT is an early stage incubator with an aim to give wings to innovative, powerful, disruptive, game changing ideas.
AXLRT's mentors and allied resources help entrepreneurs formalise business models, design, prototype and develop product, polish and smoothen user experience, devise go to market strategies, refine their investor pitch, gain media exposure and get funding.
In short, AXLRT will work intensively to get the company into the best shape possible to take on investors and markets.
